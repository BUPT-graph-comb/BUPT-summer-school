2025年"图论与组合“暑期课程

欢迎

大家好！由北京邮电大学数学科学学院主办的“图论与组合”暑期课程将于2025年8月24日-8月29日在北京邮电大学（沙河校区）开课。本次课程聚焦于组合和极值图论前沿研究的相关工具和方法，面向研究生、博后和青年老师，旨在为老师和学生们提供学术交流平台。欢迎相关研究方向的老师及学生报名参加！

课程介绍

  • 课程 1：Tight Sets, 𝑚-ovoids, and Strongly Regular Cayley Graphs
    主讲人：向青 教授（南方科技大学）
    内容：In this series of talks, we will discuss a few constructions of tight sets and 𝑚-ovoids in finite classical polar spaces. Special emphasis will be on the recent construction of Cameron-Liebler line classes in PG(3, 𝑞). For more information, please see the document.

  • 课程 2：Constructive Proofs in Extremal Graph Theory
    主讲人：马杰 教授（中国科学技术大学）
    内容：Our discussion will explore key results in Extremal Graph Theory, from foundational theorems like Turán's and Ramsey's to modern developments and innovative methods shaping the field today.

  • 课程 3：Regularity Methods and its Applications
    主讲人：韩杰 教授（北京理工大学）
    内容：Szemeredi's regularity methods have been a central tool in the development of modern combinatorics, with exciting applications in analytic number theory, graph theory and theoretic computer science. In this short course we will provide a quick overview on the regularity method, with an emphasize on the applications.
